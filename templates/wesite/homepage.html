About us

The purpose of this activity

Smartphone has become the most popular way to take photos. However, the smartphones can be shaken and/ or the target object can be moving while taking picture. Often the photos are taken in poor lighting condition such as night or too bright daylight.

Smartphone photos are made by sophisticated machine learning algorithms, and the photo quality can be improved by giving a quality scores to sample images. That is the purpose of subjective quality assessment dataset. This platform tries to collect human perception of the quality of various photos by a psychological experiment.

The purpose of the photo quality subjective assessment dataset.

Improve the quality of Image Processing Pipeline or Image Restoration algorithms in the Smartphone.

Smartphone photography involves various algorithms, and it can be only improved by creating a dataset that can teach machine to produce higher quality pictures.

Improve the Image Quality Assessment method

Humans can instantly notice the quality of a picture, but it's a difficult task for machine. By building a dataset, we can teach machine to assess the quality of photos better.

How the experiment is conducted

1.This experiment uses parallel comparison method.

2.The compared photos are processed with different algorithms of the same scene.

3.Please focus on the technical quality of the photo rather than aesthetic beauty of the picture content.
